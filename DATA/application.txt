An application can refer to various things depending on the context. Here are a few possible meanings:

1. **Software Application**: A software application is a program or set of programs that perform a specific task or set of tasks on a computer or mobile device. Examples include Microsoft Office, Google Chrome, and social media apps.

2. **Job Application**: A job application is a form or process through which an individual applies for a job or position at a company. It typically includes a resume or CV, cover letter, and other relevant documents.

3. **College or University Application**: A college or university application is a process through which an individual applies to attend a higher education institution. It typically includes a completed application form, transcripts, letters of recommendation, and standardized test scores.

4. **Mobile Application**: A mobile application, also known as a mobile app, is a software application that runs on a mobile device such as a smartphone or tablet. Examples include games, productivity apps, and social media apps.

5. **Application Development**: Application development is the process of creating a software application. It involves designing, coding, testing, and deploying the application.

6. **Application Server**: An application server is a software or hardware platform that hosts and manages applications, providing services such as security, scalability, and reliability.

7. **Application Portfolio**: An application portfolio is a collection of software applications used by an organization to manage its business processes.

8. **Application Form**: An application form is a document or online form that an individual completes to apply for a job, college or university, or other opportunity.

9. **Application Cycle**: An application cycle is the period of time during which an individual can apply for a job, college or university, or other opportunity.

10. **Application Fee**: An application fee is a payment required by an individual to apply for a job, college or university, or other opportunity.

These are just a few examples of what an application can refer to. The meaning of the term will depend on the context in which it is used.
